Repositories with ideas!

OK, so this is an unreasonable ugly website, but this is not the point (and hey! it is responsive!). The point is that this is a bunch of repositories containing ideas which are released under the Creative Commons license. This means that everybody are free to use them, but that previous collaborators needs to be credited.

Why?

People might ask "why? Why would anybody put up their good ideas for everybody to use?"

The answer is simply, that some people have too many ideas. Often ideas take offset in a piece of software that somebody wants, but they don't actually have the time to implement it. This is the chance to let everybody with enough time create fantastic applications.

Furthermore some people have troubles administering their time. This is an opportunity to get some of their future ideas
